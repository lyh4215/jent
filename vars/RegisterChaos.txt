Register a chaos policy in the current build context.

Usage:
  RegisterChaos(ChaosPolicy policy)

Behavior:
  - Adds the policy to the build-scoped chaos registry.
  - Registered policies are evaluated by Chaos(pointId) calls.

Example:
  import org.jent.chaos.ParameterChaosPolicy

  RegisterChaos(new ParameterChaosPolicy())
